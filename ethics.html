Why IT Ethics Matters

IT ethics is important because technology affects privacy, security, fairness, and public trust. Developers, data scientists, and IT professionals have a responsibility to create systems that help people while reducing harm.

Ethical Guidelines for IT Professionals

  • Protect user privacy and confidential information.
  • Avoid creating technology that causes harm.
  • Be honest about system limitations and risks.
  • Design systems that are fair and accessible.
  • Take responsibility for security and safety issues.

Ethics in Data Science

Data scientists should use data responsibly by checking for bias, protecting personal information, and making sure their work is accurate and fair. Poor data practices can lead to unfair decisions, privacy violations, and loss of trust.
